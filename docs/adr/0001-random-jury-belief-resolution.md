# Random Jury Belief Resolution

Status: accepted

TruthMarket is a random-jury belief-resolution protocol, not a fact-checking product or oracle. This decision keeps the product honest: it does not claim to discover objective truth, and it resolves markets from the revealed beliefs of randomly selected staked voters under immutable claim rules.

**Considered Options**

- Fact-checking oracle: rejected because it implies an external source of truth and makes sponsor integrations feel like truth providers.
- Prediction-style truth market: rejected as too broad and legally/product-wise fuzzy.
- Random-jury belief resolution: accepted because it matches the actual mechanism and keeps SpaceComputer randomness central.
